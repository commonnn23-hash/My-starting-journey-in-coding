  From Zero to Code: My Programming Journey

Everyone starts somewhere, but my starting line was a true "Hello World" moment. When I first began, I had **zero prior knowledge** of coding or programming logic. 
The world of syntax, compilers, and algorithms felt like a foreign language I wasn't sure I’d ever speak.

Since those first humble steps, my progress has been driven by a mix of curiosity and persistent problem-solving. 

What started as a confusing maze of brackets and semicolons has evolved into a structured way of thinking. Here is a snapshot of my progress so far:

Transitioned from total beginner to understanding core programming logic and structures

Moved beyond theory into practical application, learning how to break down complex problems into manageable code.

I am now actively developing my skills in VSC/Phyton/java/Node.js and maybe more, turning abstract ideas into functional projects.

The journey from "no knowledge" to where I am today hasn't always been linear, but every bug fixed and every concept mastered has brought me closer to my goal of becoming a proficient developer.
